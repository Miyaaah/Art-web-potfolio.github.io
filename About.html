ABOUT ME

Welcome to my Art Portfolio!

I’m a 17-year-old self-taught artist who has been passionate about drawing since I was little. I really started taking it seriously at 10, so I have 7 years of experience now. Art isn’t just my work... it’s my hobby and my joy! It’s my way of creating, expressing myself, and connecting with others. If you’re looking for help with a project or, I’d love to work with you!

My Artstyle

My art style is heavily influenced by anime, drawing inspiration from various East Asian artworks. I typically use simple flat colors and soft shading to give my pieces a clean, vibrant look. While my style isn’t always consistent, it still carries a recognizable charm that reflects my personal touch.
